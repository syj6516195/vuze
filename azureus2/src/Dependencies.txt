In order to compile this Azureus source you will need : 

SWT, available at http://www.eclipse.org 
PircBot, available at http://www.jibble.org
Systray for Java, available at http://systray.sf.net


NOTE: If you are compiling the web/web2/console UI code,
you will also need:

Log4j, http://apache.mirrors.pair.com/jakarta/log4j/
Commons-CLI, http://apache.oregonstate.edu/jakarta/commons/cli/binaries/cli-1.0.zip
SEDA, http://azureus.sourceforge.net/CVS/seda.zip
